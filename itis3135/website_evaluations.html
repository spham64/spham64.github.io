Website Evaluations

Good Website: Arc'teryx

Description: Arc'teryx is a high performance outdoor equipment company known for leading innovations in climbing, skiing and alpine technologies.

  • Contrast:
    • There are clear distinctions between the buttons, text, and background images
    • Important text are larger than less important text
  • Repetition:
    • The header stays the same throughout the entire website
    • Differing product pages have the same layout and feel
  • Alignment:
    • Products are evenly spaced out
    • Margins are consistent throughout the website
  • Proximity:
    • Products are grouped together by categories making it easier to browse and shop
    • White space is effectively used to separate sections

Assessment: Arc'teryx implements the CRAP principles in a clean and professional way. On each page there are clear distinctions between items, consistent repetition, well-structured alignment, and proximity that enhances readability and navigation.

Bad Website: IONGAF

Description: IONGAF is an online apparel brand known for its unique and limited-edition clothing items.

  • Contrast:
    • On the product pages, the black text is hard to see against the product image
    • The buy now box has edges at the top left and right while the bottom edges are rounded
  • Repetition:
    • On the homepage, the text is placed at random everytime the website is reloaded
    • Differing product pages have the same layout and feel
  • Alignment:
    • The buy now button is stuck to the bottom middle of the screen
    • Alignment of sections such as product description could be better to improve visual flow
  • Proximity:
    • Not much grouping between products as the products are limited
    • Most of the user interaction is grouped at the center bottom of the page

Assessment: IONGAF demostrates basic prinicples of CRAP but lacks in a lot of areas. Although the website is different and amusing, some of the design choices are not user friendly. The black text is sometimes hard to read. There is random placement of text on the homepage making it difficult to follow. There is good alignment and proximity is good but limited as the page is fairly basic.
