PerpetualSol Project Overview

Website Plan

  • This website is being developed to promote PerpetualSol, a self-run yoga company by Lisa Monty. This website will serve as a platform to showcase Lisa's approach to yoga and wellness.
  • The inteded users are individuals who are seeking yoga and lifestyle classes.
  • The website will have a home page, about page, classes/services page, schedule page, and a contact page.

Page Design

Home Page

  • Purpose: Main landing page
  • Audience: All visitors
  • Content: Welcome message and slideshow of images
  • Interactivity: Image slideshow

About Page

  • Purpose: Provide background information about Lisa and PerpetualSol
  • Audience: Those who want to learn more about Lisa and her company
  • Content: Personal information, mission statement, goals

Classes Page

  • Purpose: Provide information about yoga classes
  • Audience: Those who want to see what classes are taught
  • Content: Class description, images, and booking
  • Interactivity: JS that hides all classes except the one you have clicked

Schedule Page

  • Purpose: Provide information about class times
  • Audience: Those who want to see when classes are taught
  • Content: Interactive calendar and booking
  • Interactivity: Calendar

Contact Page

  • Purpose: Provide contact information
  • Audience: General inquiries from visitors
  • Content: Get in touch form
  • Interactivity: Form submission via AJAX
